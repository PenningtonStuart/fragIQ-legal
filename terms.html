4. Description of Service

FragIQ is a personal coral frag management application designed to help reef hobbyists and coral farmers track their coral specimens. The App allows you to:

  • Track coral frags with species, strain, and lineage information
  • Log health observations, weight measurements, and growth data
  • Manage tank information and water parameters
  • Upload and store photos of your corals
  • Schedule and track maintenance events
  • View trends and analytics about your collection

The App is intended for personal tracking purposes only and does not facilitate the sale, trade, or exchange of coral specimens.
