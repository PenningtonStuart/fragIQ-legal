# Beta Signup Form

The landing page includes beta signup forms for Android (Google Play) and iOS (TestFlight) testers. Form submissions are collected via a Google Apps Script backend.
- ✅ No third-party services have access to the data
- ✅ HTTPS encryption in transit

---

## Updating the Form

If you need to update the deployed script:

1. Make your changes in Apps Script
2. Click **Deploy** → **Manage deployments**
3. Click the pencil icon ✏️ to edit
4. Change version to "New version"
5. Click **Deploy**

The URL stays the same, so no changes needed on your website.
