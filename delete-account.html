Account & Data Deletion Request

Request Account Deletion

We respect your right to control your personal data. If you would like to delete your FragIQ account and all associated data, you can submit a deletion request by emailing us.

How to Request Deletion

To request the deletion of your account and data, please send an email to:

fragg3d.reef.innovation@gmail.com

Please include the following information in your email:

  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Email address: The email address associated with your FragIQ account
  • Display name: Your display name in the app (if known)

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently delete:

  • Your account profile information (email, display name)
  • All your tank data and settings
  • All your coral frag records, including health logs and notes
  • All photos you have uploaded
  • All water parameter logs and environmental data
  • All event and maintenance records
  • Any other data associated with your account

Processing Time

We will process your deletion request within 30 days of receiving your email. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been permanently deleted.

Please note that once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. Make sure to export or save any information you may need before submitting your deletion request.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the deletion process or your data, please contact us at fragg3d.reef.innovation@gmail.com.
